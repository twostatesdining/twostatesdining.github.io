About Us

who we are

A Family business originating from Hyderabad and Kerala in South India . We wanted to share our love for the south Indian cuisine with others so they can also enjoy the delights of this region.

Our Chef and team combine the intense and aromatic flavours of Indian spices to cook and serve a tasty treat of South Indian food and other Indian dishes. We strive to prepare tasty Indian food using the finest high-quality local produce. The aromatic flavours along with the aesthetic appeal of the traditional Indian food are offered to delight our customers. Come join us for a treat.

What we Offer

Authentic Spices

You are invited for a dining experience with a touch of class and evening you won't forget. We specialise in delicious and reasonably priced Indian cuisine, your food will be prepared with care using only the freshest ingredients.

Freshest Ingredients

At 2 States, we have put together a selection, where spices make their presence felt across a variety of dishes, each true to its native origins. The combination of spices transforms each dish into a leaping maze of colours, flavour and aroma.

Exquisite Flavour

Whether you are in the mood to indulge in something new or just want to enjoy some old favourites, we promise that our inventive menu and attentive service will leave you truly satisfied.

Healthy Eats

Dining at 2 States we ensure high standards of food quality and hygiene at all times, bringing you authentic, low fat and naturally cooked food of the healthy delicacies from India.

Catering and Events

Two States's offers the same delectable authentic cuisine at the restaurant to private events. Its award winning chefs and courteous, trained staff bring the same award winning experience to your special event, birthday parties, wedding reception and corporate events.

Excellent Service

We look forward to share our passion for good food and excellent service with you, and welcome you as our guests. We are proud of the time and effort that goes into preparing each of our dishes and look forward to serving you at Two States's
